#1 - Amaran (2024)

An amazing thriller staring Sivakarthikeyan as Major Mukund Vardarajan, in the Kashmir conflict.

#2 - Leo (2023)

Directed by the amazing Lokesh Kanagaraj, a wonderful thriller with Parthiban trying to mask his past as a
gangster known for illegally selling drugs, and his memories coming to haunt him.

#3 - Avatar (2009)

One of the best movies of all time, director James Cameron introduces us to the future, into the world of Pandora.
